Detailed Description

V0 particle (Lambda) reconstruction algorithm.

Authors
Oleksii Lubynets, Viktor Klochkov, Ilya Selyuzhenkov, Susanne Glaessel

Simplified version of KFParticleFinder class. At the current moment it is developed to reconstruct 2- and 3-body-decays.
SimpleFinder is based on KFParticle package, it uses mathematical apparatus implemented in KFParticle. The basic idea and reconstruction algorithm also reproduce the KFParticle, but SimpleFinder is free of overloading of too complicated procedure as in KFParticleFinder.
Also the advantage of SimpleFinder is that reconstruction procedure is clear and under full control of user, almost in the "hand mode". It gives a possibility of detailed analysis of V0 reconstruction, in particular of decay parameters distributions in order to optomize selection criterias (cuts).
